RPS 1.0


RPS (Resistant Procrustes Software) implements a compact and integrated framework to perform a resistant shape analysis of 2D and 3D configurations landmark datasets. RPS main functionalities include:
  • a generalized resistant Procrustes superposition
  • a coherent resistant distance to quantify the resulting shape differences, and
  • a resistant Multidimensional Scaling to obtain the corresponding ordination.
Additionally, least squares counterparts for all of them have also been included; both approaches can therefore be applied to the same dataset in order to compare their results.
