RPS 1.0

RPS (Resistant Procrustes Software) implements a compact and integrated framework for landmark-based resistant shape analysis of 2D and 3D datasets.

RPS main functionalities are a set of resistant tools:
i) a generalized resistant Procrustes superposition
ii) a coherent resistant distance to quantify the resulting shape differences, and
iii) a resistant Multidimensional Scaling to obtain the corresponding ordination. Least squares counterparts have also been implemented; both approaches can be therefore applied to the same dataset in order to compare their results.

RPS modular design will allow other resistant-type morphometric and statistical functionalities to be incorporated in the near future
